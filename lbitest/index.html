Outcasts, misfits, scofflaws, Ne'er do wells, anarchists - We never grew up, we sleep under the stars, we fight pirates, live for adventure, and can't stop making games!

Welcome to Lost Boys Interactive and prepare to embark on a gaming odyssey! As a remote-first video game developer, our talented team spans across the United States and Canada, delivering inspiring and captivating AAA experiences on the PlayStation, Xbox, and PC platforms. As a wholly owned yet independent subsidiary of Gearbox Software and a proud member of the Embracer Group, we thrive in a vibrant and collaborative gaming ecosystem. Get ready to join our crew of visionaries as we push the boundaries of gaming and create unforgettable adventures for players worldwide!

At Lost Boys, we make magic, nothing less!

Whether it is through our amazing art and animation, well-crafted code, incredible audio, clever designs or our commitment to quality assurance, we provide amazing experiences in the games we deliver.

Our exceptional team embraces magic, passion, creativity, and playfulness, fueled by a fulfilling work-life balance. Join us and be part of an extraordinary journey where imagination knows no bounds!

Breaking the rules is a requirement of making great art, and great mischief!

We are unapologetically remote first, allowing our talented staff across the US and Canada to work from wherever they call home. Every day, we strive to be the studio we always dreamed of working for, infusing our actions with unwavering dedication. With daring creativity and an unyielding spirit, we shatter boundaries to transform our visions into reality.

Together, we'll challenge conventions, forge new paths, and bring captivating experiences to life. At Lost Boys Interactive, the thrill of mischief awaits!

We scrap, we strive, we fight, we fly!

At Lost Boys Interactive, passion ignites our creations. We navigate the challenges of the creative process together, guided by a value of treating others as we would ourselves. Join us in a community that celebrates collaboration, respect, and unwavering support. Together, we reach greatness!

Quality Benefits

Our benefits include comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage, along with a 401k plan and life insurance options that provide safe harbor for you and your family. We take pride in promoting physical, mental, and dental health, so you can worry less and focus on unleashing your creative powers! Generous PTO plans, dedicated maternity and paternity leave, and more await you as you embark on this extraordinary journey with us. Join our crew and enjoy a bounty of perks that will fuel your passion and reward your hard work!

A Diverse Crew

At Lost Boys Interactive, diversity reigns and creativity thrives! We may make a little mischief but we take care of our own. LBI is a safe place for anyone and everyone to create. Join our crew and be celebrated for who you are. We are a dynamic and inclusive team, united by our love for game development. No matter your background, gender, orientation, or beliefs, you'll find a welcoming space here. Embrace your uniqueness and contribute to our vibrant tapestry of talent. Together, we'll create unforgettable experiences that inspire players worldwide. Come aboard and be part of our extraordinary journey!

Outcasts, misfits, orphans, scofflaws, ne'er-do-wells, anarchists - we never grew up, we sleep under the stars, we fight pirates, live for adventure, and can't stop making games.

Welcome to Lost Boys Interactive. We're a remote first video game developer with our talented staff around the United States and Canada delivering amazing AAA experiences on the PlayStation, Xbox and PC platforms. With a headquarters office in Madison, Wisconsin, Lost Boys Interactive is a wholly owned but independent subsidiary of Gearbox Software and part of the larger Embracer Group.

Lost Boys Interactive was founded in 2017 by industry veterans Shaun Nivens, Mark Stefanowicz and Rod Runnheim, with the executive team expanded in 2020 by industry stalwarts Chase Jones and Timothy Gerritsen. Combined, the group has several decades of industry experience, having worked on blockbuster games ranging across the shooter, adventure, sports, RPG, sandbox and card game genres.
